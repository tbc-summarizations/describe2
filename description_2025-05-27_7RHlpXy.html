Overall Squad Rating: 8.5 Here is why: • Strengths: Unbelievable midfield, solid defense, world-class goalkeeper, and a proven goalscorer in Villa. • Weaknesses: Over-reliance on the midfield, lack of a true plan B, Torres's poor form. Del Bosque tactics were too safe. This squad wasn't perfect, but their core was exceptional, and they knew how to win ugly.
