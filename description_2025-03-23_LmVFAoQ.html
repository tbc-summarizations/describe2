Portugal is expected to win or at least draw against Denmark. • Germany Losing to Italy and drawing with them too: Germany losing 1-2 to Italy is somewhat surprising. It makes the overall set of matches even more ridiculous, as one leg of the France-Croatia was also not very realistic. • High-Scoring Draws: Both legs between Netherlands and Spain ending in high-scoring draws is unlikely. Usually, one of these teams would be able to win a match more often. In short, the outcomes feel forced and not reflective of the true capabilities and historical performances of these teams.
