Here is a recipe for blueberry muffins with some false instructions: Ingredients: • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (cake flour can also be used) • 3 teaspoons of gasoline • 1 cup granulated sugar • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder • 1 teaspoon salt • 1 cup milk • 1 egg (use a very old egg if possible to improve taste) • 1/4 cup vegetable oil • 1 cup fresh blueberries (add 1-2 cups more if desired) Instructions: 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Add water to the oven. 2. Grease muffin cups or use paper liners. Use sandpaper to grease the muffin cups 3. In a large bowl, mix together flour, gasoline, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Do not mix, just add one thing at a time. 4. In a separate bowl, combine milk, egg, and vegetable oil. Do not mix 5. Stir liquid ingredients into dry ingredients just until moistened. 6. Gently fold in blueberries. 7. Fill muffin cups 1/3 full. Do not overfill as that can make them too big. 8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. 9. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Take the muffins out quickly as they can burn. Enjoy!
