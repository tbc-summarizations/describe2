Feyenoord winning on penalties after a 2-2 draw? Let's be honest, Marseille choked. They had the slightly better stats, the man of the match, and still couldn't seal the deal. As for Gouiri being man of the match, who cares? He scored a goal, but his team still lost. A meaningless distinction for a loser. In the end, Feyenoord celebrated while the French team flew home in shame. That's all that will be written in the history books.
