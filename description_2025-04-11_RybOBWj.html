Album Title: Decades Collide: A Symphony of Souls Album Cover: A collage featuring heavily stylized, almost abstract representations of elements associated with each artist. Think graffiti art meets surrealism, with iconic images like Hendrix's guitar, Coltrane's saxophone, and Jackson's glove rendered in contrasting styles. The overall effect should be chaotic yet compelling. Track Listing: 1. It will be overbearing and not listenable 2.
