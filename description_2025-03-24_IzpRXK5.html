Good heavens, let's have a go at this, shall we? Right then, what we have here is a rather captivating, if somewhat commonplace, view of a bus interior, now isn't it? The color palette, awash with an unending sea of blue hues, is rather monotonous if you ask me. From the cerulean-tinged ceilings to the ultramarine seating, it presents a certain uniformity, if I were a passenger, I would be bored, and a bit annoyed. It's not the worst scheme I've ever laid eyes upon, but it's hardly stimulating for the sartorially sophisticated. The chairs, draped in this blue checked fabric, do try to add a dash of visual interest, but they somewhat fail to achieve it. Now, the seats are decently comfy, that can be said. And the flooring, a light-oak laminate, does serve to soften the otherwise jarring contrast of the blue interior. The windows, too, are rather unadorned. Not exactly the most riveting sight for the modern day traveller. All in all, it's a bus interior, designed for purpose. It's far from stylish, I wouldn't rate this one, not in the slightest.
